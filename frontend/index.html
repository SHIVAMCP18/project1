Core Features

Everything your
warehouse needs

01
📦

Create and manage products with SKU codes, categories, units of measure, and initial stock values.

📥

Move stock between warehouses or production floors. Total quantity unchanged — location updated.

Inventory Flow

How stock moves
through your business

1
Receive from Vendor
Create a receipt, add supplier & products, validate. Stock increases automatically.
2
Internal Transfer
Move stock where it's needed — Main Store → Production Rack. Total unchanged, location updated.
3
Deliver to Customer
Pick, pack, validate. Stock decreases on dispatch. Fully tracked with status history.
4
Adjust & Reconcile
Damaged goods, count mismatches — log an adjustment. Everything posted to the Stock Ledger.
